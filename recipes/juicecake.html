The orange juice cake recipe

Orange Juice Cake

This cake is similar to a rum cake, but you substitute orange juice for rum.

Ingredients:

  • 1 (3.5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
  • 4 eggs
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup cold water
  • ½ cup butter
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • ¾ cup orange juice
